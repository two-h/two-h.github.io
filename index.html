Quallity, consistant, coffee that your shop can depend on

Suppling coffee for cafes, bars and restarunts is our core business, and its one that we know and do well. The owners of Two H started their coffee journies making espressos for and running speciallity coffee shops, and have a combied 10 years of experience in that trade.

To that end, we at Two H believe delisious coffee should be unprotentious and accsessable. This is why we are upfront with our pricing here on our website, and too our knowlage are the only roastery to use a volume pricing model, offering savings to our customers where ever we can is important to us. We also invite or clients along to the roastery, Where they are able to bring their staff and get hands on with the roasting process. The idea being that they would then be able to take enthusiam and knowlage back to work with them and share with thier customers. We beielve these are amoungst the things that set us apart as a company. As ultimatly building relationships, especially in the service industry, is key to our combinded success.


Something special for the kitchen cupboard

We know how important that morning brew is so all our coffee's are available in smaller bags for kitchen cupboards up and down the country. We also offer a contiunously changing small batch coffee, along side our espresso & decaf coffee, giving the home enthusiast the abillity to experence single origin coffees from all over the world.
