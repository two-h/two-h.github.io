Our Coffee's

At Two H we believe in simplcity and our offering for you reflects this. We source our green stock to roast & blend each of our coffees to be the very best they can be, fit for purpose. Our house blend will always be, what we believe, everything an espresso coffee should be. Classy, Rich and Sophisticated. Our Decaf Coffee will never compromise on flavour and always use a natrual, non chemical, decafination process. Sometimes we come across coffees that are truely exceptional, with real personalities, and when we do we feel obliged to share them with you. We call this our Showcase coffee and this it always show everything a coffee can be.
The beans the make up our three coffee's will change over time, with the seasons and availability, but each will always be true to these beliefs.
