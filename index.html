OUR BUISNESS

We got you covered!

  • WholeSale

Quallity, consistant, coffee that your shop can depend on

Suppling coffee for cafes, bars and restarunts is our core business, and its one that we know and do well. The owners of Two H both started their coffee journies making espressos for and running speciallity coffee shops, and have a combied 10 years of experience in that trade. We learnt a few things during this time, that have shaped our ethos and the way in which we conduct ourselves and our buisness.

To this end we believe delisious coffee should be unprotentious and accsessable. This is why we are upfront with our pricing right here on our website, not being hidden behind a vetting process. As well as this We, too our knowlage, are the only roastery to use a volume pricing model. Offering savings to our customers where ever we can is important to us.

We also love to invite or clients along to the roastery, Where they are able to bring their staff and get hands on with the roasting process. The idea being that they would then be able to take any enthusiam and knowlage back to work with them and share with thier customers.

We believe these are amoungst the things that set us apart as a company. As ultimatly building relationships, especially in the service industry, is key to our combinded success.


Something special for the kitchen cupboard

We know how important that morning brew is, well every brew, so all our coffee's are available in smaller bags for kitchen cupboards up and down the country.

As well as our Espresso's coffees, we offer a contiunously changing limieted edition coffee. This coffee will be a little more expensive than the others, but it will always be coffee with a truely distingused personallity and flavour profile. The idea being to give home enthusiast the abillity to experence single origin coffees from all over the world, and taste the differences between Countries, Regions, Vertials and Processes.


Truly uniquie coffees & branding to make your shop or project stand out

Although its true to say all our customers are fellow collaborators sometimes we are approched with unique and exciting ideas that need a personal touch. Where we work together with our clients to create unique coffee's and branding. For example: we've created special blends for espresso based cocktails, we have created bespoke coffee packaging to match our customers own brand identity, and sponcered a film maker too produce a nature documentry.

We believe this openess to clients ideas and projects are more of the things that set us apart.




OUR COFFEE

The line up !

Hopefully by now its clear how at Two H we believe in simplicty and accessability, and wanted to offer a clear and consise range of coffees for our customers, that refelcts this. We are proud to introduce our line up to you, three coffees carefully selected to be the best that they can be.

Our 'Seasonal Espresso' will always make, what we believe, everything an espresso coffee should be: Classy, Rich and Sophisticated. We sell the most of this, our customers love its consisentcy and ability to take milk whilst maintaining a distinctive character. To achive this we do lots of cupping, blending and tasting. We even consider the phyical shape of green beans as this can affect the eveness of the roast and its very important to us to keep consistency up.

Our 'Seasonal Decaf Espresso' shares the same standards as our Seasonal Espresso just without the caffine. There are a few ways of extracting caffine from coffee beans, broadly speaking a cheap way and a more expensive way, and we will only use a natrual, chemical free, decafination process or the more expensive way. We dont however pass the full preminum onto our customers, its just important to us to do it this way !

We wanted to provide a platform for people to exprernece coffees that have truely distingused personallities and charatisitics. So we created our 'Limited edition' coffeee. In these bags will be coffees that showcase the amazing differences between between Countries, Regions, Vertials and Processes that you can find in single origin beans. Perfect for the home enthusist or guest grinders.

The beans that make up our coffees will change over time, with the seaosns & supply, but they will always be true to these beilfs.
