OUR BUISNESS

Quallity, consistant, coffee that your shop can depend on

Supplying coffee wholesale for cafés, bars & restaurants is our core business, and it is one we know well. The owners of Two H both started their coffee journeys as baristas & running speciality coffee shops, and have a 10 years of combined experience in that trade. During this time we have learnt a few things that have helped create our ethos and shaped our attitude towards coffee and the way we like to do business.

As a result we believe great coffee should speak for itself and be accessible to everyone. This is why we are transparent and upfront with our pricing, right here on our website, for everyone to see and with no hidden costs. We believe our volume pricing model is unique in the market , making sure that our coffees are available to all customers, big or small, at a fair & competitive price. It also enables us to offer savings to our customers on larger orders, which was something that we never came across when we were buying roasted coffee.

We also believe that successful business is built on great relationships and working together with our customers. That is why we love to invite or clients along to the roastery, where they are able to bring their staff and get hands on with the roasting process. The idea being that they would then be able to take the knowledge & enthusiasm that we share with them, back to their workplace which they can then pass onto their customers.

These values and attention to detail are truly important to us, and are how we believe we can offer the best service to our customers, not just by providing delicious & great value coffee, but by building lasting relationships together. Which is ultimately the key to our joint succsess.


Something special for the home

We know how important that morning brew is, well every brew, so all our coffee's are available in smaller bags for kitchen cupboards up and down the country.

We know that everyone has their favourite type of coffee, but also understand that every now and then you want to try something new. That is why, in addition to our espresso coffees, we offer our limited edition range – coffees that we really feel the need to share with you. These will continuously change as we discover beans worth sharing, however they will always be premium coffees with a truly distinguished personality & flavor profile. The idea being to give the home enthusiast the opportunity to experience single origin beans from all over the world and taste the differences between countries, regions, varietals & processes.


Truly unique coffees & branding to make your business stand out

Although it is true to say all our customers are fellow collaborators, sometimes we are approached with unique and exciting ideas that need a personal touch. This is where we work together with our clients to create unique coffee blends and branding to create a completely tailor made product and premium service.

To give one example; we have created special complimentary blends for a coffee & cocktail bar. One blend for the day when serving coffees, the other for use at night whilst serving coffee themed cocktails. Along with bespoke packaging to match the theme of day & night really added value to their brand, and they have had great feedback subsequently.

Projects like this are something that we really enjoy, and find incredibly satisfying. We believe our willingness to engage with clients ideas, and bring them to life, are amongst some of the things that set us apart as a company.




OUR COFFEE

The line up

Hopefully by now it’s clear how at Two H we believe in simplicity, and we want to offer a clear and concise range of coffees for our customers that reflects this.

Our 'Seasonal Espresso' will always make, what we believe, everything an espresso coffee should be: Classy, Rich and Sophisticated. Our bestseller. Our customers love its consistency and ability to take milk whilst maintaining a distinctive character, something important to any coffee business. To achieve this we do lots of cupping, tasting & experimenting with blends. We even consider the physical shape of green beans as this can affect the evenness of the roast and it’s very important to us to keep consistency up.

Our ‘Decaf Espresso' shares the same standards as our Seasonal Espresso just without the caffeine. There are a few ways of extracting caffeine from coffee beans, broadly speaking a cheap way and a more expensive way, and we will only use a natural, chemical free, decaffeination process or one that does not detract from the taste and quality of the bean. This is the more expensive way, however we do not pass the full premium onto our customers, it is important for us to offer this option and make it available to everyone.

We wanted to provide a platform for people to experience coffees that have a truly distinguished personality and characteristics. So we created our 'Limited edition' coffee range. In these bags will be coffees that showcase the amazing differences between Countries, Regions, Varietals and Processes that you can find in single origin beans. Perfect for the home enthusiast or guest bean for your business.

The beans that make up our coffees will change over time, with the seasons & supply, but they will always be true to these beliefs.
