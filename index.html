COFFEE'S

OUR COFFEE'S

AtTwo H we believe in simplcity and offer three, carefully profiled, coffees fit for purpose. Our Seasonal Espresso beans will always make, what we believe, everything an espresso coffee should be. Classy, Rich and Sophisticated. Our Blue Label decaf will never compromise on flavour and always use a natrual, non chemical, decafination process. Sometimes we come across coffees that are truely exceptional, with real personalities, and when we do we feel obliged to share them with you. We call this our Showcase coffee, you can find them in our Red Label bags. The beans the make up our three coffee's will change over time, with the seasons and availability, but each will always be true to these beliefs.
