OUR BUSINESS

Hello friends, welcome to our website. We are Two H , a Coffee Roasting company. Selecting, roasting and sending out delisious coffees for our fellow coffee lovers is what we do. Over the next few pages we hope to show you how we like to do things: Simply , Honestly and Friendly. Explore the below tabs to see what we can do for you.


Quallity, consistant, coffee that your shop can depend on

Suppling coffee for cafes, bars and restarunts is our core business, and its one that we know and do well. The owners of Two H started their coffee journies making espressos for and running speciallity coffee shops, and have a combied 10 years of experience in that trade. During this time we learnt a few things that we always keep in mind when delevoping new espresso blends:

  • Your Baristas, and your customers, need consistancy in their cups.
  • Most customers have milk in their drinks.
  • Buyers of roasted coffee need a stable price upon which to build their buisness.

    • We at Two H believe delisious coffee should be unprotentious and accsessable. This is why we are upfront with our pricing here on our website and why we invite or clients along to the roastery. Where they are able to bring their staff and get hands on with the roasting process, to then be able to pass that enthusiam and knowlage to thier customers. This is a really enjoyable part of the job for us as ultimatly building relationships, especially in the service industry, is key to our combinded success.

    OUR COFFEE'S

    At Two H we believe in simplcity and our offering for you reflects this. We source our green stock to roast & blend each of our coffees to be the very best they can be, fit for purpose. Our Green Label coffee will always make, what we believe, everything an espresso coffee should be. Classy, Rich and Sophisticated. Our Blue Label decaf will never compromise on flavour and always use a natrual, non chemical, decafination process. Sometimes we come across coffees that are truely exceptional, with real personalities, and when we do we feel obliged to share them with you. We call this our Showcase coffee, you can find them in our Red Label bags. The beans the make up our three coffee's will change over time, with the seasons and availability, but each will always be true to these beliefs.
