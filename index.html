Our Buisness

Quallity, consistant, coffee that your shop can depend on

Suppling coffee for cafes, bars and restarunts is our core business, and its one that we know and do well. The owners of Two H started their coffee journies making espressos for and running speciallity coffee shops, and have a combied 10 years of experience in that trade.

To that end, we at Two H believe delisious coffee should be unprotentious and accsessable. This is why we are upfront with our pricing here on our website, and too our knowlage are the only roastery to use a volume pricing model, offering savings to our customers where ever we can. We also invite or clients along to the roastery, Where they are able to bring their staff and get hands on with the roasting process. The idea being that they would then be able to take enthusiam and knowlage back to work with them and share with thier customers.

We beielve these are amoungst the things that set us apart as a company. As ultimatly building relationships, especially in the service industry, is key to our combinded success.

Something special for the kitchen cupboard

We know how important that morning brew is so all our coffee's are available in smaller bags for kitchen cupboards up and down the country. We also offer a contiunously changing small batch coffee, along side our espresso & decaf coffee, giving the home enthusiast the abillity to experence single origin coffees from all over the world.

Truly uniquie coffees to make your shop or project stand out

OUR COFFEE

We got you covered!

At Two H we believe in simplcity and offer three, carefully profiled, coffees fit for purpose. Our Seasonal Espresso beans will always make, what we believe, everything an espresso coffee should be. Classy, Rich and Sophisticated. Our Blue Label decaf will never compromise on flavour and always use a natrual, non chemical, decafination process.Sometimes we come across coffees that are truely exceptional, with real personalities, and when we do we feel obliged to share them with you. We call this our Showcase coffee, you can find them in our Red Label bags. The beans the make up our three coffee's will change over time, with the seasons and availability, but each will always be true to these beliefs.
